Organization of business meetings and meetings

For the success of a business event, in addition to organizational efficiency, it is necessary to successfully match the format of the meeting and its objectives. It is equally important that the work plan maintains a balance between the business itself and the relaxation and entertainment parts.

1

The topic should be interesting to all participants.

2

Mutual trust of partners in negotiations is necessary.

3

Those in attendance need the authority to make decisions, as well as the training to do it skillfully.

4

The conversation should stipulate and take into account the objective and subjective interests and wishes of all parties, and a willingness to compromise should be shown.
